About Me

My name is Justin and I am from Seattle, WA. I was born and raised here, so I miss our Sonics but love our Seahawks (GO HAWKS). I am a long time student of life.. and of the University of Washington, but decided to take some time off to enroll in this coding bootcamp! I have always been into computers/technology in general, so I felt that this would be an exciting challenge for me to take on. I really enjoy creating and building things, whether that be computer, car or even kitchen related. I try to maintain an active lifestyle by playing sports and working out consistently, while also making time for listening to music, reading, watching movies/tv shows and playing video games.

My favorite music genre is Hip-Hop/Rap, but I listen to a variety of genres. I tend to read a lot of fiction books, with one of my favorites being The Wind-Up Bird Chronicle by Haruki Murakami. My all-time favorite show is HBO's 'The Wire,' and I can't say I have a favorite video game, but I play a lot of shooters and RPGs. I have two dogs which are crazy and I also like drinking beer, so I am always down to drink after class! Looking forward to the next 5.5 months of this bootcamp.
